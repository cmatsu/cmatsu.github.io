Observing Machine Spaces of Digital Infrastructures

Final capstone for MSGT program. Used ArcGIS Pro, ArcGIS Online, and Mapbox to create an ArcGIS StoryMap that shows the spatial and socioeconomic disparities of areas in Seattle occupied by digital infrastructures.
Using Hexbins for Density Mapping

Used ArcGIS Pro and Mapbox to compare point cluster with hexbin mapping techniques with data of fire occurrences across the US and the state of Florida from 1984 to 2018.
Schools and McDonalds in Tacoma, WA

Designed a map showing the locations of schools and McDonalds to assess children's accessibility to fast food. Used Mapbox for stylizing the map, and all icons for this map were designed using Procreate and Inkscape.

Flood Risk Vulnerability in Sugar Land, TX

Designed an interactive map using ArcGIS Pro and ArcGIS Online to compare FEMA flood risk data with Social Vulnerability Index data in the city of Sugar Land, Texas.

Mapping Me

Designed a map that tells a spatial story of where I lived growing up, inspired by Hong Kong's subway system maps using Procreate. Every line represents a country I lived in and every stop represents a memorable location.

Commission for Gecko Afterlife

Designed cover art and created a mascot character for independent musician Gecko Afterlife's artist persona using Procreate. The character design was later evaluated in a character sheet style piece of artwork.

Racial and Social Equity in Seattle

Designed a website featuring interactive equity maps created with Mapbox. These maps were designed to represent spatial analysis of racial and socioeconomic Seattle census tract data.

Income and Medical Access in King County

Designed a website featuring interactive choropleth maps created with Leaflet.js. This map shows the relationship between median income and medical facility access based on King County census data.

A History of the Music Scene in Capitol Hill

Designed a self guided tour to showcase the history of Capitol Hill's music scene using WordPress. The theme of this page was inspired by the iconic rainbow crosswalks of the neighborhood.

Transit Accessibility in Low-Income Neighborhoods

Designed an informative map poster showing public transit accessibility in low income census tracts. Used ArcGIS to develop a Transit Accessibility Factor and generated maps to reflect which areas received the most frequent and proportional service.
