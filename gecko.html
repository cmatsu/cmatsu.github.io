Commission for Gecko Afterlife

Gecko in Fantasia cover art


Mascot character sheet


I was given the opportunity to design the album cover art for artist Gecko Afterlife's newest release, Gecko in Fantasia. Using Procreate, I created a gecko mascot character to feature in the artwork and developed the final album art around it. Afterwards I received another opportunity to expand on this character design by creating a character sheet style illustration.
