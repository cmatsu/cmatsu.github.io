Hi, I'm Chiaki. Welcome to my portfolio!

I'm a graduate student at UW Tacoma studying Master's of Science in Geospatial Technologies set to graduate in August of 2021. I previously graduated from UW Seattle in Spring 2020 with a Bachelor of Arts in Geography.


My interests are designing solutions to enhance user experiences, as well as creating maps that illustrate lived experiences and identify societal problems.

Using Hexbins for Density Mapping

Used ArcGIS Pro and Mapbox to compare point cluster with hexbin mapping techniques with data of fire occurrences across the US and the state of Florida from 1984 to 2018.
Schools and McDonalds in Tacoma, WA

Designed a map showing the locations of schools and McDonalds to assess children's accessibility to fast food. Used Mapbox for stylizing the map, and all icons for this map were designed using Procreate and Inkscape.
Flood Risk Vulnerability in Sugar Land, TX

Designed an interactive map using ArcGIS Pro and ArcGIS Online to compare FEMA flood risk data with Social Vulnerability Index data in the city of Sugar Land, Texas.
Racial and Social Equity in Seattle

Designed a website featuring interactive equity maps created with Mapbox. These maps were designed to represent spatial analysis of racial and socioeconomic Seattle census tract data.
