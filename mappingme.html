Mapping Me

Final subway inspired map


Designed a map that tells a spatial story of where I lived growing up, inspired by Hong Kong's subway system maps using Procreate. The map features a fictional railway with service lines each representing a country that I lived in and the stops represent cities that are the most meaningful or memorable to me.
